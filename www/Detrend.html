Detrend time series

 The 
'Detrend'
 module supports time series detrending (see 
'trend' note
 at the bottom of the page).

 The 
'Detrend method'
 can be set as :

   
'Seasonal decomposition'
  - seasonal decomposition by Loess.

   
'Differencing'
            - first order differencing.

   
'Smoothing'
               - Loess smoothing.
                  
 Click 
'Detrend'
 button to perform data detrending. 
 The detrended data will be plotted and taken for further analysis.

 The help window appears when 
'Help'
 button is pressed. Click the 
'Close'
 button to close the window.

 On an error, the error window could appear (see 
'Error window' warning
 at the bottom of the page). 

 
Notes:

  
'trend': Double meaning of the word 'trend'.
 
   Google trend data are time series data. In statistic, the trend is only a component of a time series 
   that represents variations of low frequency. The double meaning of the word 'trend' may cause a misunderstanding. 
   In order to avoid it, 'google trend' will be referred here as 'google time series' or 'traces'.

 
Warnings:
 
  
'Error window': Error window closing.

   To close an error window please click 
'Close please'
 button.
   Otherwise the window remains open even if the error disappeared.
